Australian Signals Directorate Work Experience Program for Cyber Security and Roboics
Every School Holidays, the Australian Signals Directorate holds a work experience program for students in years 11 and 12. If you're really talented, they might take you in year 10, but that rarely happens. I was fortunate enough to take part in it during the mid-year, end of semester break in 2023, when I was still a year 11 student.
